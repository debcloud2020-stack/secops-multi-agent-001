# NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 — control summaries (paraphrased)

## GOVERN (GV)
Establish and monitor the organization's cybersecurity risk management strategy,
expectations, and policy. Roles, responsibilities, and authorities are defined.

## IDENTIFY (ID)
Maintain an inventory of hardware, software, and services. Asset management and risk
assessment determine which systems (e.g., internet-facing gateways) are critical.

## PROTECT (PR)
Apply safeguards: identity management and access control (least privilege, MFA),
data security, and platform security including timely patching of known vulnerabilities.
Network segmentation isolates high-risk assets from the rest of the environment.

## DETECT (DE)
Continuously monitor networks and identities for anomalies; analyze events such as
failed-signin bursts, impossible travel, and unexpected privilege grants.

## RESPOND (RS)
Execute the incident response plan: triage, contain (isolate affected hosts), eradicate,
and communicate. Preserve evidence and coordinate stakeholders.

## RECOVER (RC)
Restore assets and operations after an incident; incorporate lessons learned into the
recovery and response plans.
