# ISO/IEC 27001 Annex A — control summaries (paraphrased)

## A.5 Organizational controls
Policies for information security, segregation of duties, and supplier relationships.
Threat intelligence is collected and analyzed to inform controls.

## A.8 Technological controls
- A.8.2 Privileged access rights are restricted and reviewed; grants of administrator
  roles are tightly controlled and monitored.
- A.8.8 Technical vulnerabilities are identified and remediated within defined
  timeframes (patch SLA); exposure of unpatched, internet-facing services is minimized.
- A.8.16 Monitoring activities detect anomalous behavior and potential incidents.
- A.8.20/8.22 Network security and segmentation separate critical systems.

## A.5.24–A.5.28 Incident management
Plan, prepare for, and respond to information security incidents; assess and decide on
events; collect evidence; learn from incidents.
